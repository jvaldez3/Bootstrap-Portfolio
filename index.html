About Me

Josh was born and raised in Utah. He grew up in Lehi and graduated from Lehi High School in 2010. Some of Josh's hobbies are theater, hunting, traveling, bowling, playing video games, and watching baseball. Some of Josh's favorite places he's traveled include Iceland, England, Australia, Fiji, and Italy.

Josh has a degree in Theater Performance from Utah Valley University and performs at HaleCenter Theater in both Sandy and Orem. Some of his favorite shows he has been in are Peter Pan, In the Heights, Newsies, and Catch Me If You Can. Josh works at CompHealth as a Sr. Hospital Privileges Coordinator. He currently lives in Bluffdale, with his husband Michael, and his two huskies that he is obsessed with.
